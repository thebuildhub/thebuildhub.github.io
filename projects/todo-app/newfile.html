    Remember The created list "do not get destroyed" when you refresh or exit the page. All the content is stored in broswer itself.
    So you get the list as it is when you open the page next time from same browser.(opening in different browser will not show same list).
    Your list is private to you and you only as it is stored in your broswer and not send to server.

    How to Use

    1. You can store the category of things you want to do in 'Things to do' section.
      like Grocery
    2. You can select this list by simply clicking on it.
    3. Then you can add new items to this selected list by adding it the box the appear below
      like 1.bring milk , 2.bring cheese etc
    4. Then you can click on the items once they are complete(the checked item will show a line of cut through them). You can see the task remaining in the upper right corner of the box.
    5. You can delete the tasks complete by clicking on "clear completed task" botton below the box
    6. You can also delete a complete list by clicking on "Delete list" button on the bottom right of box
